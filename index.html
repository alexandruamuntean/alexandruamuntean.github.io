About me
or how I fell in love with web development

My work experience is quite eclectic. I've done graphic design, studied civil engineering, built fireplaces, and cooked professionally. Last year I decided to become a web developer. "Why?" you might ask. Well, I want to combine my passion for learning, creative thinking, problem-solving and hard work into one while having fun doing so.

Ever since I decided to pursue a career in web development there hasn't been a day without me coding. I'm simply in love with the rush of solving a programming problem and seeing the final result.
